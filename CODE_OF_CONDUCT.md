# Code of Conduct

This project expects contributors and maintainers to keep collaboration
respectful, direct, and reviewable.

## Expected behavior

- be respectful in technical disagreement
- focus critique on the data, code, or process rather than the person
- cite sources and assumptions clearly
- accept review feedback in good faith
- help keep the corpus trustworthy by flagging unclear provenance or rights issues

## Unacceptable behavior

- harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- discriminatory language or exclusionary behavior
- knowingly submitting data you do not have the right to contribute
- concealing provenance, fabrication, or other deceptive research practices

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, reject, or remove contributions that violate these rules.
Repeated or severe violations may result in removal from project spaces.

If you need to report a problem, contact the project maintainer privately
through the repository owner’s listed contact channel.
